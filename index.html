About Flam Train Station

Welcome to our guide to the Flam Station in Norway! Nestled amidst the breathtaking fjords, Flam Station is not only a hub for transportation but also a marvel of architecture, blending modern design with traditional Norwegian elements. This iconic building serves as the starting point for the famous Flam Train journey, renowned for its panoramic views and engineering feats. Beyond the station, Flam town offers a wealth of activities for visitors to enjoy. You can explore the charming streets lined with colorful wooden houses, visit local museums to learn about the area's history and culture, or embark on a fjord cruise to marvel at the stunning natural beauty up close. For the adventurous souls, Flam is also a popular starting point for hiking and cycling trails, offering endless opportunities to immerse yourself in Norway's picturesque landscapes. Whether you're seeking relaxation or adventure, Flam has something for everyone, making it a must-visit destination in Norway.

The Journey in Norway

The Flam Train journey is an unforgettable experience that spans approximately 20 kilometers (12 miles) in distance, taking travelers on a scenic voyage through some of Norway's most breathtaking landscapes. The journey is typically completed aboard the Flam Railway, a renowned model of railway engineering celebrated for its steep gradients and numerous tunnels. On board, passengers can expect comfortable seating and large windows, offering uninterrupted views of the stunning surroundings. As the train winds its way from Flam to Myrdal, passengers are treated to a visual feast of towering mountains, cascading waterfalls, and verdant valleys. Some of the notable landmarks along the route include the majestic Kjosfossen waterfall, the picturesque Aurlandsfjord, and the dramatic Flam Valley. While the Flam Train journey is enchanting year-round, the best time to embark on this adventure is during the summer months when the landscapes are lush and vibrant, and daylight hours are longer, allowing for more time to soak in the stunning scenery.

Plan Your Flam Adventure

  1. Plan Your Travel Dates: Decide on the dates you want to travel to Flam from Oslo. Consider factors like weather, availability of accommodations, and any special events happening in Flam during your visit.
  2. Purchase Train Tickets: Visit the official website of the Norwegian State Railways (NSB) or use a reputable online booking platform to purchase train tickets from Oslo to Flam. Make sure to select the appropriate travel dates and class of service based on your preferences.
  3. Check Train Schedule: Verify the departure and arrival times of the trains traveling from Oslo to Flam. Note any connections or transfers you may need to make along the route.
  4. Arrive at Oslo Central Station: On the day of your journey, arrive at Oslo Central Station well in advance of your train's departure time. This will give you ample time to locate your platform and board the train comfortably.
  5. Board the Train: Once your train arrives at Oslo Central Station, board the train and find your assigned seat or cabin. Stow your luggage securely and settle in for the scenic journey to Flam.
  6. Enjoy the Train Ride: Sit back, relax, and enjoy the picturesque views as the train travels through the stunning Norwegian countryside. Keep your camera handy to capture the breathtaking landscapes along the way.
  7. Arrive at Flam Station: After several hours of travel, the train will arrive at Flam Station, nestled in the heart of the fjord region. Gather your belongings and disembark from the train.
  8. Explore Flam: Take some time to explore the charming village of Flam and its surrounding natural beauty. Visit attractions such as the Flam Railway Museum, stroll along the waterfront, or embark on a fjord cruise to fully experience the magic of Flam.
  9. Book Return Tickets: If you're planning a round trip from Oslo to Flam, be sure to book your return train tickets in advance to secure your preferred travel dates and times.
  10. Depart from Flam: On the day of your departure, arrive at Flam Station in time to board your return train to Oslo. Bid farewell to Flam and cherish the memories of your unforgettable journey through the Norwegian wilderness.

Norwegian Delicacies to Try

  • Smoked Salmon: Indulge in the rich flavor of traditional Norwegian smoked salmon, often served with creamy scrambled eggs or on freshly baked bread.
  • Brunost (Brown Cheese): Sample this unique Norwegian cheese with a sweet caramel flavor, perfect for pairing with crackers or crispbread.
  • Kjøttboller (Norwegian Meatballs): Savor these flavorful meatballs served with lingonberry sauce and creamy mashed potatoes, a classic Norwegian comfort food.
  • Rømmegrøt (Sour Cream Porridge): Warm up with a bowl of creamy sour cream porridge, topped with a dollop of butter and sprinkled with cinnamon and sugar.
  • Aquavit: Enjoy a taste of Norway's signature spirit, Aquavit, a distilled beverage flavored with herbs and spices such as caraway, dill, and citrus.
  • Knekkebrød (Crispbread): Snack on traditional Norwegian crispbread, topped with cheese, cured meats, or your favorite spreads for a satisfying bite on the go.
  • Cloudberries: Treat yourself to the delicate sweetness of cloudberries, a wild berry native to Norway, often enjoyed fresh or in jams and desserts.
  • Kvikk Lunsj: Grab a Kvikk Lunsj, a popular Norwegian chocolate bar similar to a KitKat, perfect for a quick and tasty snack during your journey.
  • Akevitt: Toast to your Norwegian adventure with a shot of Akevitt, a traditional Scandinavian spirit often enjoyed as a celebratory drink.
